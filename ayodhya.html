Best Places to Visit

Ram Janmabhoomi

A historic temple complex, believed to be the birthplace of Lord Ram.

Kanak Bagicha

A temple dedicated to Lord Ram, set in a beautifully landscaped garden.

Treta Yuga

A prominent spiritual site connected to the Ramayana epic.

Best Foods to Try

Ayodhya Kebabs

Succulent kebabs made with tender marinated meat.

Chaat

A spicy and tangy street food snack enjoyed by locals.

Lassi

A sweet yogurt drink, perfect for refreshing on a hot day.

Local Cultural Festivals

Diwali

Diwali in Ayodhya is celebrated grandly with a massive display of lights and festivities.

Ram Navami

The birthday celebration of Lord Ram, a significant event for devotees.

Holi

Celebrate the festival of colors in this spiritual city.
