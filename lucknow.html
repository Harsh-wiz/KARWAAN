Best Places to Visit

Bada Imambada

One of the largest arched constructions in India, Bada Imambada is an iconic historical monument.

Chota Imambada

A stunning piece of architecture, known for its beautiful chandeliers and intricate designs.

Ambedkar Park

Public park and memorial, dedicated to Dr. B.R. Ambedkar featuring grand architecture, statues, and lush green landscapes.

Best Foods to Try

Tunday Kebab

A must-try for non-vegetarian food lovers, known for its unique preparation.

Lucknow Biryani

A delicious blend of flavors, this biryani is rich and aromatic.

Sheermal

A sweet and fragrant bread that is perfect with kebabs and curries.

Local Cultural Festivals

Eid Festival

The Eid celebrations in Lucknow are grand, with prayers, food, and festivities.

Navratri

Experience cultural dance and music during this nine-day festival in honor of Goddess Durga.

Diwali

The festival of lights is celebrated with grand decorations, sweets, and fireworks.
