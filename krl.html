Best Places to Visit

Backwaters

The serene backwaters of Kerala are famous for houseboats and natural beauty.

Munnar

Famous for its tea gardens and lush green hills, a hill station worth visiting.

Alleppey

A picturesque town known for its houseboat cruises on the backwaters.

Best Foods to Try

Sadya

A traditional Kerala feast served on a banana leaf, featuring various curries and rice.

Appam

A soft, rice-based pancake, often served with stew or curry.

Karimeen

Fried pearl spot fish, a famous Kerala delicacy.

Local Cultural Festivals

Onam

The grand harvest festival of Kerala, celebrated with dances, feasts, and boat races.

Vishu

The New Year festival celebrated with traditional rituals and a grand feast.

Thrissur Pooram

A spectacular temple festival with grand processions and fireworks.
