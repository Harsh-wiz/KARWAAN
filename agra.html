Best Places to Visit

Taj Mahal

One of the Seven Wonders of the World, Taj Mahal is the epitome of love and Mughal architecture.

Agra Fort

A UNESCO World Heritage site, this massive fort was the seat of the Mughal Empire.

Fatehpur Sikri

A historic city and a UNESCO World Heritage site built by Emperor Akbar.

Best Foods to Try

Petha

A sweet delicacy made from pumpkin, unique to Agra.

Agra Biryani

Spicy and aromatic biryani made with tender meat and fragrant rice.

Mughlai Kebabs

Flavourful kebabs made with marinated meat, a Mughal specialty.

Local Cultural Festivals

Diwali

The festival of lights is celebrated with great enthusiasm across Agra.

Holi

Agra celebrates Holi with colors, music, and traditional sweets.

Eid

Eid celebrations are marked with prayers, feasts, and festivities.
