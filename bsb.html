Best Places to Visit

Kashi Vishwanath Temple

A famous Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, located on the banks of the Ganges.

Dashashwamedh Ghat

The most prominent and busiest ghat on the Ganges river, famous for its evening aarti.

Sarnath

A Buddhist pilgrimage site where Lord Buddha delivered his first sermon.

Best Foods to Try

Varanasi Kachori

A spicy and crispy snack, served with chutneys.

Banarasi Lassi

A traditional yogurt drink, served in large earthen pots.

Pani Puri

Popular street food with spicy water and crispy puris.

Local Cultural Festivals

Dev Deepawali

Celebrated with grand lights and offerings to the Ganges.

Mahashivaratri

Devotees fast and pray at Kashi Vishwanath Temple on this auspicious night.

Holi

The festival of colors is celebrated with much joy and excitement.
