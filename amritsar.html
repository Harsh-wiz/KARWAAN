Best Places to Visit

Golden Temple

The spiritual center of Sikhism, known for its golden facade and serene atmosphere.

Jallianwala Bagh

A historic garden commemorating the tragic massacre of 1919.

Wagah Border

Witness the electrifying border ceremony between India and Pakistan.

Best Foods to Try

Amritsari Kulcha

A delicious stuffed bread served with spicy chickpea curry.

Lassi

A creamy yogurt-based drink topped with fresh cream.

Makki di Roti and Sarson da Saag

A traditional Punjabi dish loved for its earthy flavors.

Local Cultural Festivals

Vaisakhi

Celebrate the Punjabi New Year with joy and traditional dances.

Gurpurab

Commemorate the birth anniversaries of Sikh Gurus with devotion.

Lohri

A harvest festival celebrated with bonfires, folk songs, and dances.
