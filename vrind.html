Best Places to Visit

ISKCON Temple

A famous temple dedicated to Lord Krishna, attracting devotees from around the world.

Banke Bihari Temple

One of the most revered temples of Lord Krishna, known for its unique rituals and aarti.

Prem Mandir

A stunning temple complex dedicated to Radha-Krishna, illuminated beautifully at night.

Best Foods to Try

Lassi

A refreshing yogurt-based drink, often served with cream and dry fruits.

Malpua

A sweet pancake soaked in sugar syrup, enjoyed by locals and visitors alike.

Peda

A delicious milk-based sweet, often offered as prasad in temples.

Local Cultural Festivals

Holi

Experience the most vibrant and colorful Holi celebrations in the land of Krishna.

Janmashtami

Celebrate the birth of Lord Krishna with grandeur and devotion.

Radhastami

Dedicated to Radha, this festival is celebrated with devotion and joy.
