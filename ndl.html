Best Places to Visit

India Gate

A war memorial honoring soldiers, surrounded by lush green lawns and an iconic sight in Delhi.

Red Fort

A UNESCO World Heritage site symbolizing the grandeur of Mughal architecture.

Qutub Minar

The tallest brick minaret in the world and an iconic historical landmark.

Best Foods to Try

Chole Bhature

A spicy chickpea curry served with fluffy deep-fried bread.

Paratha

Stuffed flatbreads served with yogurt, pickles, and butter.

Butter Chicken

A creamy tomato-based chicken curry, a must-try delicacy in Delhi.

Local Cultural Festivals

Republic Day Parade

A grand celebration showcasing India's cultural heritage and military prowess.

Diwali

The festival of lights celebrated with joy, sweets, and fireworks.

Lohri Festival

A traditional Punjabi festival celebrated with bonfires, dance, and music.
